The Beautiful Game: Soccer

Introduction to Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in most parts of the world, is the most popular sport globally. Played by over 250 million players in more than 200 countries, it brings together people of all ages and backgrounds.

The Rules of Soccer

Soccer is a game played between two teams of 11 players. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal. The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.

  • Each match lasts for 90 minutes, split into two halves.
  • Players are not allowed to touch the ball with their hands, except for the goalkeeper.
  • A goal is scored when the ball completely crosses the goal line.
